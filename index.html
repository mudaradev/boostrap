About Us

Sign for Deaf is a project designed to help individuals with hearing impairments communicate more easily. The goal is to create a sensor glove that can translate sign language into spoken or written text. This technology enables deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals to communicate effortlessly with the hearing world. The project combines hardware (a glove with sensors) and software (real-time translation on a web platform) to create a simple and effective solution. Our main objective is to make communication easier and more accessible for people with hearing disabilities. As an undergraduate team, we've worked together to integrate AI and machine learning to improve the accuracy and efficiency of the translation system. Through this project, we aim to enhance the daily lives of individuals with hearing impairments and raise awareness about the importance of digital accessibility for everyone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make the world more accessible for people with hearing impairments. By developing a real-time sign language translation system, we aim to break communication barriers and help individuals engage in education, work, and social life. We are committed to improving communication and creating more opportunities for deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals. Our solution focuses on providing equal access to information and services, promoting inclusivity, and showing how technology can enhance the lives of people with disabilities.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build an inclusive digital world for individuals with disabilities, ensuring that accessibility and equal opportunities are provided across all sectors of society. We aim to foster a society that supports and integrates everyone regardless of their abilities.
